## Notes / Uncertainty Flags

- **Cube Controls "Universal Clutches"** is a single accessory SKU but explicitly fits F-Pro, CSX-3, GT-X2, and GT Pro V2 — applied across all four entries.
- **MOZA Paddles Add-on Kit** is one common 6-paddle upgrade module compatible across the GS V2P / FSR2 / KS / Vision GS / CS Pro lineup — applied across MOZA entries.
- **Ascher Gen6 magnetic paddle force-adjust kit (260–800g)** appears to be standard inclusion across all F28/F64/Artura wheels rather than a separately-purchased kit.
- **GSI AH50 hub** is mentioned for Hyper P1 explicitly; assumed compatible with the rest of the GSI lineup but not explicitly verified per-model.
- **Conspit MagFlip / Enduro grip** — both wheels share architecture; documented for both 290GP and 300GT.
- **Sim-Lab Mercedes F1 2024** (#51) — official licensed wheel; no separate paddle/grip add-ons found beyond per-button RGB. Uncertainty flagged.
- **HRS GT8-BT** (#56) — entire wheel is build-to-order with rim choice as the primary "accessory"; no published modular kits beyond that.
- **Simucube Valo GT-23 grip** (#3) — "Alcantara" and "Leather" appear to be sold as separate complete-wheel SKUs, not interchangeable grip kits.
